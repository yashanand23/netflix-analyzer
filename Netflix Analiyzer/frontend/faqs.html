Frequently Asked Questions

+ What is Netflix Analyzer?
- Netflix Analyzer is a platform that allows users to explore top movies and TV shows on Netflix, view ratings, find trending content, and get detailed information about different shows.
+ Who developed Netflix Analyzer?
- Netflix Analyzer was developed by three tech enthusiasts. They hold strong passion to create solution to existing problems with the help of technology. Completed their B.Tech Degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the same college in 2024.
+ How do I use the dashboard?
- The dashboard provides a user-friendly interface to browse and analyze various Netflix shows and movies. You can filter content by genre, region, and ratings.
+ Is Netflix Analyzer affiliated with Netflix?
- No, Netflix Analyzer is an independent project and is not affiliated with Netflix, Inc.
+ Are there any subscription fees for using Netflix Analyzer?
- No, Netflix Analyzer is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges associated with our platform. Simply visit our website and start exploring the world of Netflix entertainment today!
+ How accurate are the ratings and reviews on Netflix Analyzer?
- The ratings and reviews on Netflix Analyzer are sourced from a dataset obtained from Kaggle, a reputable platform for datasets and data analysis. Before incorporating the dataset into our platform, we conducted thorough data cleaning and analysis techniques to ensure accuracy and reliability. While we strive to provide the most accurate information possible, it's important to note that ratings and reviews are subjective and may vary based on individual preferences and opinions.
